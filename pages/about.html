Our Journey

From the Problem We Lived

01
Where We Started

Born From the Problem We Lived

Before DALA became what it is today, we were you. We had a company that was slowly dying. Not because the product wasn't good. Not because customers didn't want it. But because the operational chaos of Nigerian retail was eating us alive.

Deliveries were killing our cash flow. Getting into stores felt impossible. Small order quantities weren't worth it to logistics companies, so we stayed stuck in a handful of locations while competitors expanded.

That's when DALA was born — not as a business idea, but as a lifeline.

02
The Breakthrough

What We Learned Changes Everything

DALA helped Wilson Lemonade scale from struggling in a few stores to thriving in over 300 modern retail locations across Lagos and Ogun. What took us close to a decade to achieve on our own could have been done in 1–2 years with what we know now.

We learned: the problem was never the product. It was the operations. When you can't deliver efficiently, you can't scale. When payments are unpredictable, you can't plan.

We lived that reality. And we found the way out.

03
What We Do Today

Sharing What Saved Us

Today, DALA exists to help other founder-led brands move faster than they ever could alone. We help you get on more shelves, expand across Lagos and Ogun, and scale rapidly — while you focus on what actually matters: making great products and building demand.

We're not selling you theory. We're sharing what saved us. This is tested, proven, and works because we've lived it, fixed it, and built a system that helps brands like yours achieve in 1-2 years what used to take a decade.
