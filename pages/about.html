The Struggle

Nigerian retail challenges

Where We Started

Born From the Problem We Lived

Before DALA became what it is today, we were you. We had a company that was slowly dying. Not because the product wasn't good. But because the operational chaos of Nigerian retail was eating us alive.

Deliveries were killing our cash flow. Getting into stores felt impossible. Small order quantities weren't worth it to logistics companies.

That's when DALA was born — not as a business idea, but as a lifeline.

02

The Breakthrough

From 10 to 300+ stores

The Breakthrough

What We Learned Changes Everything

DALA helped Wilson Lemonade scale from struggling in a few stores to thriving in over 300 modern retail locations across Lagos and Ogun.

What took us close to a decade to achieve on our own could have been done in 1–2 years with what we know now.

We learned: the problem was never the product. It was the operations. When you can't deliver efficiently, you can't scale.

03

Today

Helping brands scale

What We Do Today

Sharing What Saved Us

Today, DALA exists to help other founder-led brands move faster than they ever could alone. We help you get on more shelves, expand across Lagos and Ogun, and scale rapidly.

While you focus on what actually matters: making great products and building demand.

We're not selling you theory. We're sharing what saved us.

Why Brands Trust Us

Five reasons why leading brands choose DALA as their retail execution partner.

01
Local Expertise

We Understand Nigerian Retail Realities

We're not theory-driven consultants. We operate daily in Lagos and Ogun's retail environment. We know the challenges, the politics, the delays — and we've built systems that actually work here.

02
Guaranteed Payments

We Guarantee What Matters Most

Payment stress kills brands. That's why we guarantee remittance within agreed timelines, even when retailers delay. You get predictable cash flow so you can plan and grow with confidence.

03
Full Visibility

We Operate With Full Transparency

You receive weekly reports on sales, inventory, store activity, and issue resolution. You're never in the dark about what's happening with your brand. No vague updates. Just clarity.

04
Peace of Mind

We Protect Your Time and Energy

When you partner with DALA, you get your life back. No more chasing payments. No more coordinating deliveries at midnight. You focus on building the brand. We handle the engine.

05
Structured Execution

We Turn Growth Into Repeatable Execution

Expansion should not feel chaotic. We bring disciplined in-store routines, escalation paths, and operating standards that keep shelf presence, fulfilment, and reporting aligned as you scale.
