Requirements

What You Need to Get Started

To ensure quality and compliance, we require the following documents and information from all brand partners.

Legal Documentation

Valid business registration and compliance documents required for partnership.

  • CAC Certificate of Registration
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • NAFDAC Registration (for food products)
  • Product Liability Insurance

Product Standards

Products must meet quality standards and have consistent supply availability.

  • Minimum 6 months shelf life
  • Consistent product quality
  • Adequate inventory capacity
  • Proper packaging and labeling

Business Operations

Established operational capacity to handle retail distribution at scale.

  • Active business for 12+ months
  • Dedicated account manager
  • 48-hour order fulfillment
  • Return policy in place

Financial Readiness

Demonstrated financial stability to support retail expansion.

  • Business bank account
  • 30-day payment terms acceptance
  • Credit references available
  • Minimum monthly revenue ₦500K

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does the approval process take?

Our standard review process takes 48-72 hours after receiving your complete application. If additional information is needed, we'll reach out within 24 hours.

What are the fees for getting listed?

We operate on a commission-based model. There are no upfront listing fees. We earn when you sell, aligning our success with yours.

Can I start with a trial period?

Yes! We offer a 3-month trial period with select retail partners to test market fit before full rollout across our network.

What support does DALA provide?

We provide end-to-end support including store onboarding, in-store execution, sales reporting, payment collection, and dedicated account management.

How do I track my sales performance?

You'll receive weekly sales reports via email and have access to our partner dashboard for real-time performance tracking across all store locations.
