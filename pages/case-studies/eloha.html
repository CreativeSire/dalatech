The Challenge

Launch Success & Rapid Market Entry

Eloha needed to launch successfully and build market presence quickly. As a new brand entering modern retail, they required structured execution and disciplined operations to establish credibility and drive revenue from day one.

Without an established retail track record, Eloha faced the challenge of convincing supermarkets to stock their products while simultaneously building consumer awareness. They needed a partner who could execute flawlessly from the very first day.

The Solution

Complete Launch Management

DALA managed Eloha's retail launch and growth from May through November 2025. We handled store onboarding, execution, and performance tracking, enabling the brand to scale to significant market presence within seven months.

Our comprehensive approach included coordinated launch and store expansion to 83 retail locations, managed in-store execution and product visibility from day one, provided consistent replenishment to support growing demand, and delivered transparent reporting on revenue growth and market performance.

Launching with DALA meant we got it right from day one. Their execution gave us the credibility we needed to scale fast.

— Eloha Leadership Team
The Results

Strong Growth from Day One

Eloha experienced consistent and strong revenue growth following its mid-year launch. The partnership successfully launched the brand and scaled it to significant market presence within just seven months, building a stable and growing income stream.
