The Challenge

Scaling Revenue & Retail Presence

Zayith Food Company had quality products but needed to scale revenue and expand retail presence. The brand required structured execution support to accelerate growth and reach more customers across modern retail outlets.

Without a coordinated retail strategy, Zayith was missing opportunities to convert product quality into commercial momentum. They needed a partner who could handle the operational complexity while they focused on product development.

The Solution

Comprehensive Retail Operations Management

DALA managed Zayith's retail operations focusing on revenue acceleration and market expansion. We handled store onboarding, retail operation execution, reports and performance tracking, enabling the brand to scale effectively.

Our team coordinated product onboarding and expansion into new retail locations, managed in-store execution and product visibility, supported seasonal campaigns with disciplined execution, and provided transparent reporting on revenue and performance trends.

Quality products need quality execution. DALA helped us turn our product advantage into real market presence and revenue growth.

— Zayith Leadership Team
The Results

Peak Performance Achieved

Zayith Food Company experienced remarkable revenue growth throughout the fiscal year. Performance built steadily and peaked in the later months, indicating effective seasonal campaigns and scaled marketing efforts that significantly boosted earnings.
