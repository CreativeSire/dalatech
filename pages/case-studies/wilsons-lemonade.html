Wilson's Lemonade

Scaling Wilson's Lemonade Through Structured Retail Execution

Wilson's Lemonade moved from fragmented delivery demand to disciplined retail expansion with stronger shelf presence, clearer reporting, and a repeatable operating rhythm.

₦221.7M revenue generated 350 retail stores 1.5 years to scale
Wilson's full lemonade range now appears together on shelf, making the story feel specific to the products shoppers actually meet in-store.
Challenge

The growth demand was there, but distribution was holding it back.

Wilson's Lemonade had demand, but distribution was limiting growth. Orders came in from different locations in small quantities, making delivery expensive and unsustainable. Limited retail presence and operational inefficiencies slowed expansion.

Solution

DALA became the retail execution layer behind expansion.

DALA stepped in to coordinate store onboarding, strengthen in-store execution, support campaign visibility, and provide transparent reporting. The focus was to turn scattered demand into a system that could scale with discipline.

What We Did

Store Onboarding

Coordinated product onboarding across 350 retail locations.

In-Store Execution

Managed shelf placement and product visibility.

Campaign Support

Supported seasonal promotions and launches.

Transparent Reporting

Provided real-time revenue and performance data.
