The Challenge

Distribution Limitations and Operational Inefficiencies

Wilson's Lemonade had demand, but distribution was limiting growth. Orders came in from different locations in small quantities, making delivery expensive and unsustainable. At the same time, limited retail presence and operational inefficiencies slowed expansion and kept the team focused on logistics instead of scaling.

The Solution

How DALA Helped

DALA took over Wilson's retail and distribution operations, removing the logistics burden and creating a structured path to scale. We helped the brand expand into more supermarkets, coordinated deliveries, and managed store-level execution—allowing Wilson's to grow without operational strain.

What We Did

  • Expanded distribution into multiple retail locations
  • Managed store onboarding, logistics, and merchandising
  • Provided clear sales and performance reports
  • Ensured consistent product availability across stores
The Results

The Results

Wilson's lemonade achieved major revenue generation and expanded its retail network significantly within approximately 1.5 years. The brand demonstrated strong and consistent sales growth throughout the partnership, reflecting successful product adoption and increasing market demand.
