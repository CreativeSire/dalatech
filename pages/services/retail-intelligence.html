Understanding the Modern Retail Landscape

In today's competitive FMCG market, having access to accurate, timely retail intelligence is the difference between growth and stagnation. DALA's Retail Intelligence service provides brands with comprehensive data and insights that illuminate the path to retail success across Lagos and Ogun State.

What We Provide

  • Market Analytics: Detailed performance data across 500+ retail locations, tracking sales trends, stock levels, and consumer behavior patterns.
  • Competitor Intelligence: Comprehensive analysis of competitor positioning, pricing strategies, and shelf presence in target retail outlets.
  • Store Performance Tracking: Individual store-level insights showing which locations drive the highest returns for your brand.
  • Category Insights: Understanding of category dynamics, seasonal trends, and emerging opportunities in your product segment.
  • Weekly Reports: Automated reporting delivered to your inbox with actionable recommendations based on the latest data.

How Brands Use Our Intelligence

Our retail intelligence empowers brands to make informed decisions about inventory allocation, promotional timing, and market expansion. Wilson's Lemonade used our data insights to optimize their store mix, focusing on high-performing locations and achieving ₦221.7M in revenue within 18 months. Zayith Food Company leveraged competitor analysis to identify pricing opportunities and expand to 173 stores.

The DALA Difference

Unlike traditional market research that provides delayed, generalized insights, DALA's intelligence is real-time and specific. We collect data directly from the retail floor through our field team, giving you an accurate picture of what's happening in stores today—not what happened last quarter. This immediacy allows you to respond quickly to market changes and stay ahead of the competition.
