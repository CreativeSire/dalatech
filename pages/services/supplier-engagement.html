Supplier Engagement

Building lasting partnerships between brands and retailers through professional engagement and support.

The Foundation of Retail Success

Strong supplier relationships are the backbone of successful retail distribution. DALA's Supplier Engagement service acts as the bridge between your brand and retail partners, ensuring smooth operations, clear communication, and mutual growth across every store in our network.

Our Engagement Approach

  • Relationship Management: Dedicated account managers who understand your brand and maintain strong connections with retail buyers and category managers.
  • Regular Store Visits: Weekly visits to partner stores to check stock levels, shelf positioning, and address any issues before they impact sales.
  • Communication Facilitation: Acting as the liaison between your brand and retailers for orders, payments, promotions, and problem resolution.
  • Performance Reviews: Quarterly business reviews with retail partners to discuss performance, opportunities, and growth strategies.
  • Training & Support: Educating retail staff about your products to improve customer recommendations and sales conversion.

Why Engagement Matters

In Nigeria's competitive retail landscape, brands that maintain active, positive relationships with stores achieve significantly better outcomes. Our engagement programs ensure your products stay top-of-mind with retailers, receive prime shelf placement, and benefit from staff recommendations. August Secrets credits their rapid expansion to 106 stores in part to our proactive engagement strategy that kept their brand visible and well-positioned.

Measurable Outcomes

Brands using our Supplier Engagement service report 40% fewer stock-out incidents, faster payment cycles, and improved shelf positioning compared to managing retailer relationships independently. We handle the day-to-day relationship maintenance so you can focus on growing your business.
