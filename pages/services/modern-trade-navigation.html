Understanding Modern Trade

Nigeria's retail sector is evolving rapidly. Modern trade—including supermarkets, convenience stores, and chain retailers—now represents the fastest-growing channel for FMCG brands. However, navigating this landscape requires specialized knowledge of retailer requirements, category management, and operational standards. DALA's Modern Trade Navigation service provides the expertise you need to succeed.

Navigation Services

  • Channel Strategy: Guidance on which retail formats (hypermarkets, supermarkets, convenience stores) best match your brand positioning and growth objectives.
  • Retailer Mapping: Comprehensive overview of Nigeria's modern retail landscape, including key players, geographic coverage, and entry requirements.
  • Operational Standards: Education on modern trade requirements including supply chain protocols, packaging standards, and technology integration.
  • Category Management: Insights into how retailers organize categories, planograms, and shelf allocation to optimize your product placement.
  • Growth Pathways: Strategic roadmap for expanding from independent stores to regional chains to national retail presence.

From Traditional to Modern

Many Nigerian FMCG brands built their business in traditional markets and informal retail. Transitioning to modern trade requires a shift in mindset and operations. We guide brands through this transformation, helping you understand the differences in buyer behavior, margin expectations, and promotional dynamics. Our navigation support ensures you enter modern trade prepared and positioned for success.

Local Expertise, National Reach

DALA's team has deep experience across Nigeria's retail ecosystem. We understand the nuances of operating in Lagos versus Ogun State, the differences between international chains and local retailers, and the unique challenges of each market segment. This expertise accelerates your learning curve and helps you avoid costly mistakes as you navigate modern trade expansion.
