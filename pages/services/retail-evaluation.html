Retail Evaluation

Assess retail opportunities, evaluate store performance, and identify growth potential for your brand.

Making Informed Retail Decisions

Not all retail opportunities are created equal. DALA's Retail Evaluation service provides brands with objective, data-backed assessments of potential and existing retail partnerships. We help you understand which stores will drive the best returns, where to focus your expansion efforts, and how to optimize your current retail presence.

Evaluation Framework

  • Store Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of store location, foot traffic, customer demographics, and operational standards.
  • Sales Potential Analysis: Category-specific modeling to forecast sales performance based on store characteristics and market data.
  • Competitive Landscape: Analysis of competitor presence, pricing, and shelf positioning within target stores.
  • Operational Review: Assessment of retailer capabilities including payment reliability, inventory management, and promotion execution.
  • ROI Projections: Detailed financial modeling showing expected returns, payback periods, and profitability thresholds.

Strategic Applications

Our evaluations inform critical business decisions. Before launching in new stores, we assess whether the opportunity aligns with your brand's margin requirements and growth objectives. For existing retail partnerships, we identify underperforming locations and develop improvement strategies or exit recommendations. Wilson's Lemonade used our evaluation framework to optimize their store mix, focusing resources on the 350 highest-potential locations and achieving superior returns.

Ongoing Performance Tracking

Retail evaluation isn't a one-time exercise. We continuously monitor store performance against projections, alerting you to changes that warrant attention. If a previously strong-performing store begins declining, we investigate root causes and recommend corrective actions. This proactive approach protects your investment and ensures your retail network remains optimized for growth.
