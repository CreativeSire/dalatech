ABOUT US
Before DALA became what it is today, we were you. We had a Company that was slowly dying. Not because the product wasn't good. Not because customers didn't want it. But because the operational chaos of Nigerian retail was eating us alive.
Deliveries were killing our cash flow. Getting into stores felt impossible. Small order quantities weren't worth it to logistics companies, so we stayed stuck in a handful of locations while competitors expanded. Every day felt like we were running harder just to stay in the same place. We were drowning, and we didn't know how to fix it.
That's when DALA was born…not as a business idea, but as a lifeline. We built the operational engine we desperately needed to save our own company. And it worked.
DALA helped Wilson Lemonade scale from struggling in a few stores to thriving in over 300 modern retail locations across Lagos and Ogun. What took us close to a decade to achieve on our own,could have been done in 1-2 years having what we know now –DALA.
We learned: The problem was never the product. It was the operations. When you can't deliver efficiently, you can't scale. When payments are unpredictable, you can't plan. When you're buried in logistics instead of marketing, your brand can't grow. We lived that reality. And we found a way out.
Now we know better. And we do better.
Today, DALA exists to help other founder-led brands move faster than they ever could alone. We help you get on more shelves, expand across Lagos and Ogun, and scale rapidly—while you focus on what actually matters: making great products and building demand. We're not selling you theory. We're sharing what saved us.
This is tested. This is proven. This works because we've lived it, fixed it, and built a system that helps brands like yours achieve in 1-2 years what used to take a decade.
If we could save our own brand from the edge, we can help you scale yours with structure and speed.
That's why DALA exists.
Who We Are
DALA is the retail operations engine for founder-led consumer brands across Lagos and Ogun. We exist to remove the operational bottlenecks that keep great products from scaling responsibly in modern retail.
We're not a traditional distributor. We're not a marketing agency. We're an operational partner built specifically for brands that want structure, transparency, and disciplined growth—without the crushing overhead and constant stress that usually comes with retail expansion.
We handle the entire order-to-payment process so you can step out of logistics and step fully into what actually drives your business forward: creating quality products and building demand.
Why We Exist
After we saved Wilson Lemonade, other founders started reaching out.They know our story, saw our results, and wanted us to help them achieve the same results too. They had great products people loved, but broken systems were holding them back—stockouts, delayed payments, delivery issues, and constant burnout. These weren’t failing businesses. They were good brands trapped by bad operations just like we were.
That’s when we realized DALA couldn’t remain just our internal engine. It had to become the operational partner we once needed. Today, DALA helps founders escape operational chaos and scale with structure.We run the retail operations engine so you can focus on what you do best—creating quality products and building demand. We handle the push (store relationships, delivery, merchandising, payment collection) while you handle the pull (marketing, product quality, brand building).
Today, DALA exists to help founders break free from operational chaos and scale with structure.
