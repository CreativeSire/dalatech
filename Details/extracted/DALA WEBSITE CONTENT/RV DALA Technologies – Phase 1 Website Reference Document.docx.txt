5. About Us Page:
Reference websites: 
Asana (https://asana.com), Squarespace (https://www.squarespace.com)
We want the About Us page to feel clean, professional, and premium, like Asana and Squarespace. Use a combination of concise text sections and visuals (images or icons) to tell our story, highlight mission, vision, values, and the brands we serve on the about us page. The layout should guide the reader naturally from “Who we are” → “What we do” → “How we add value” → “Join us/Call-to-action.  Keep it approachable, human, and trustworthy, with clear spacing and visual hierarchy for easy reading.
